Our services include:

At Qnine, we offer a wide range of professional cleaning services designed to meet the unique needs of businesses and individuals across the Netherlands. From maintaining a spotless office to handling industrial cleaning challenges, we provide tailored solutions that ensure clean, hygienic, and welcoming spaces. Explore our specialized services below to learn how we can help you achieve the highest standards of cleanliness.

Office Cleaning:

Qnine offers comprehensive office cleaning services to maintain a clean, professional work environment. Our team handles daily or periodic cleaning tasks such as dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and sanitizing workstations, restrooms, and common areas. We ensure that your office space remains spotless, creating a productive and hygienic atmosphere for employees and clients.

Industrial Cleaning:

For large-scale industrial environments, Qnine provides specialized cleaning services designed to meet the unique needs of factories, warehouses, and production sites. Our experienced team cleans heavy machinery, floors, and high-traffic areas, removing industrial dirt, grease, and debris. We use advanced equipment and cleaning techniques to ensure the highest standards of cleanliness and safety in your facility.

Window And Facade Cleaning

Qnine offers expert window and facade cleaning to keep your building looking its best. Whether it's interior or exterior windows, our team uses advanced tools and eco-friendly cleaning products to deliver streak-free results. We also provide facade cleaning services, ensuring the exterior of your building is spotless and well-maintained, enhancing both appearance and durability.
